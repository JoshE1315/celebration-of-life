Privacy Notice

We want you to feel comfortable responding. Here is exactly what happens with the information you share.

What we collect

Your name, your reply (attending, not attending, or tentative), the number and names of those attending, and any optional details you choose to add such as an email address, phone number, dietary needs, accessibility needs, or a short message.

Why we collect it

We use it only to plan and communicate about this Celebration of Life. For example, to arrange seating, food, and accessibility, and to reach you if plans change.

Where it is stored

Your response is stored in a private Google Sheet that only the family can open. The public web page is hosted on GitHub Pages. When you send the form, Google processes the submission through Google Apps Script and Google Sheets.

About memories and messages

Any memory or message you share may be read privately by the family. It will not automatically be posted publicly on this website.

No advertising or tracking

This site uses no advertising and no tracking cookies. We do not sell or share your information.

Corrections and deletion

You may ask the family to correct or delete your information at any time. Please use the contact details above. You can also update your own RSVP by submitting the form again with the same invitation code.
